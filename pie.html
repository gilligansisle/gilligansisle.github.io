Mary Ann's Coconut Pie

"A taste of home, even when you're three thousand miles away from Kansas!"

Ingredients

  • 1 (9-inch) Pie Crust, Baked
  • 3 Egg Yolks, Beaten
  • Dash Of Salt
  • 3/4 Cup Sugar
  • 3 Cups Whole Milk
  • 2 Tablespoons Butter
  • 1/2 Cup Cornstarch
  • 1 Cup Coconut
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 3 Egg Whites
  • Coconut For Garnish
  • Meringue for topping Preferably
  • But Whipped cream Will Do Also

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 Degrees
  2. In the top of the double boiler when the water begins to simmer over medium heat 275áµ’F, beat the egg yolks and salt until frothy.
  3. Add the sugar, milk, and butter. Mix well.
  4. When the bottom pan begins to boil whisk in the cornstarch with a water (a tablespoon at a time).
  5. Cook until thick, whisking constantly.
  6. Add the coconut and vanilla and mix well. Pour the filling into the baked pie shell and set aside. Cool for at least 1 hour
  7. In the mixing bowl, beat the egg white (adding a small amount of cream of tartar (1/4 teaspoon and 6 tablespoons of sugar at a time) to stiff peaks.
  8. Spread meringue over pie. Garnish with coconut. Bake for 8 - 10 minutes until lightly browned.
  9. Top with whipped cream instead if you want to and extra toasted coconut before serving to the Skipper!
