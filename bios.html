THE FAITHFUL TOUR

THE SEVEN CASTAWAYS

Gilligan

The First Mate

The accident-prone first mate of the S.S. Minnow. Despite his mistakes, his heart is always in the right place.

The Skipper

Jonas Grumby

The brave captain of the S.S. Minnow. He’s a natural leader who spends most of his time looking out for his "Little Buddy."

Thurston Howell III

The Millionaire

A billionaire who brought thousands of dollars for a three-hour tour. He refuses to lift a finger but loves his wife, Lovey.

Lovey Howell

Eunice Wentworth Howell

The elegant socialite who keeps the island classy. She’s the heart of the group and always has a kind word for everyone.

Ginger Grant

The Movie Star

A glamorous Hollywood starlet. She uses her acting skills to help the group (and to keep morale high).

The Professor

Roy Hinkley

The man who can build a nuclear reactor out of coconuts, but somehow can't fix a hole in a boat!

Mary Ann

The Farm Girl

A sweet girl from Kansas. She keeps the castaways fed with her famous pies and her practical common sense.
